The story

A life mapped across seven cities and three continents — it taught me to build for people who aren't like me.

I was born in Tokyo to Lebanese parents, grew up in Paris, and spent years in Dubai before crossing to California and, now, New York. Four cultures, four languages, one throughline: figuring out how things work and making them for other people.

That's why I sit at an unusual intersection — the math and the ML on one side, the product, design, and ship-it grit on the other. I like owning a problem from the model all the way to the thing in someone's hands.
